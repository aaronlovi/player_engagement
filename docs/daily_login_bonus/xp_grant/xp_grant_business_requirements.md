### Daily Login Bonus – XP Grant

**Section:** Business Requirements

**Purpose:** Define business-level expectations, goals, and constraints for how XP is granted to players through daily login interactions.

| **ID** | **Requirement Title**               | **Description**                                                                 | **Rationale / Goal**                                               | **Priority** | **Acceptance Criteria**                                                 |
| ------ | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| BR-01  | Daily XP Reward                     | Players receive XP each day they log in during the defined claim window.        | Encourage consistent daily engagement.                             | High         | XP is granted once per valid daily login.                               |
| BR-02  | Streak Incentives                   | XP amount increases based on consecutive login days (streak).                   | Create “habit loops” and long-term engagement motivation.          | High         | XP progression curve applies correctly by streak length.                |
| BR-03  | Grace Period                        | Missed-day grace policy allows maintaining a streak for a limited gap.          | Prevent player frustration and reduce churn from missed days.      | Medium       | Player streak remains active if they miss ≤ N days defined by policy.   |
| BR-04  | Fairness Across Timezones           | Players cannot double-claim by changing timezone or location.                   | Maintain fairness and prevent exploitation.                        | High         | Each user can claim XP only once per “reward day,” defined server-side. |
| BR-05  | Retention Analytics                 | Track engagement metrics (claim rate, streak retention, XP earned).             | Allow product owners to measure and tune reward efficacy.          | Medium       | System logs daily claim, streak stats, and XP totals per user.          |
| BR-06  | Seasonal Boosts                     | XP grants can be increased during special events or seasons.                    | Align player reactivation campaigns with season launches.          | Medium       | Policy allows temporary XP multipliers or event-based overrides.        |
| BR-07  | Personalization by Segment          | Different user cohorts may have different XP policies (e.g., new vs returning). | Enable A/B testing and targeted re-engagement.                     | Medium       | Operators can assign policies by player segment.                        |
| BR-08  | Anti-Abuse Controls                 | Prevent automation or multiple account claims from one user/device.             | Maintain game economy integrity.                                   | High         | Device/account/IP limits and cooldowns in place.                        |
| BR-09  | Policy Versioning                   | XP grant rules must be versioned and auditable.                                 | Support rollback, analysis, and experiment comparison.             | High         | Each XP grant references a policy version ID.                           |
| BR-10  | Visual Feedback                     | Player receives immediate UI feedback upon claiming XP.                         | Reinforce progress and satisfaction.                               | High         | XP award triggers visible UI confirmation and updated level bar.        |
| BR-11  | Integration with Progression System | XP contributes to global player level or other progression paths.               | Maintain unified sense of advancement.                             | High         | XP awarded appears in player’s progression bar and totals.              |
| BR-12  | Offline Claim Recovery              | If connection drops mid-claim, system can return same receipt later.            | Ensure reliable player experience and prevent loss or duplication. | High         | Retried claims within same day return same receipt ID.                  |
| BR-13  | Administrative Controls             | Operators can configure XP amounts, streak curves, claim windows, and segments. | Enable live-ops tuning without code changes.                       | High         | Policy parameters editable in operator console.                         |
| BR-14  | Transparency                        | Players can clearly see next reward and streak status in UI.                    | Build trust and reinforce engagement habit.                        | Medium       | Game client shows streak count, next day reward, and XP preview.        |
| BR-15  | Compliance and Fair Play            | The XP grant does not confer competitive advantage in matchmaking or PvP.       | Maintain fairness between paying and non-paying players.           | High         | XP affects only cosmetic/progression systems, not gameplay power.       |
